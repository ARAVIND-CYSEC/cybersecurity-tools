Common Cyber Threats

Phishing

Tricking users to give sensitive info via fake emails or websites. Be wary of suspicious links and always verify sender identity.

Ransomware

Malicious software that locks your data until you pay a ransom. Always keep backups and avoid clicking suspicious links.

Malware

Software designed to damage or gain unauthorized access to your system. Use antivirus software and avoid downloading untrusted files.
